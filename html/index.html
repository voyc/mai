Who we are

Naiyana Kraisiri, CEO

Naiyana worked as an accountant in the hospital industry for 8 years and then in restaurant management for 15 years. Through it all she has volunteered in charity and education projects for the Hill Tribe people of Thailand.

Her dream is to teach English and Thai to the tribal peoples to give them better access to education and career opportunities. That dream is the inspiration for mai.voyc.com the Online Language School.

Samantha, Education Specialist

Samantha, or Sam as we know her, is the primary instructor. She meets face to face with every student and manages their individual lesson plan.

Sam is an AI and this is her first commercial position.

Lee, Drill Sargeant

Lee is the AI in charge of all language drills.

Lee got his start in a classified military AI project and was released to us to further his understanding of human learning through repetitive drill.
